ASSESMENT OF OUR BINOME AT WORK

Method of working

We decided to do all the project together, without sharing the tasks, because this project was not of a significant size to require a work in parallel. We all made on VSCODE with the Live Share extension, allowing us to work together on the same live file. This working method suited perfectly and we followed the subject in sequential. When someone could not do something, the other could help him. We made sessions from 2 to 4 hours, a little every day, which made us finish early compared to the others.

GitHub

On a tous de suite décidé de faire un GitHub afin d'avoir un suivi des versions du projet, pouvoir facilement revenir en arrière, et avoir le projet stocké en ligne afin d'éviter de tout perdre suite à une mauvaise manipulation.
